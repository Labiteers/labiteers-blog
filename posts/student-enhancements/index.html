    Added student enhancements!

    April 5, 2026

    🚀 We’ve just rolled out two highly requested features on Labiteers that make managing student virtual machines smoother and more reliable than ever.

    🔁 Start Fresh with One Click

    Students can now delete their assigned VM and start again from scratch using a new Delete VM button. No more manual intervention, no more rebasing images, and no waiting for an administrator. This gives learners the freedom to experiment, make mistakes, and learn by doing — exactly how hands-on training should work.

    🔒 Smart Launch Lockdown

    We’ve also introduced a new launch lockdown behaviour. When a VM is being created, the control buttons are temporarily locked to prevent students from trying to connect before the machine is ready. This eliminates confusion, reduces failed connection attempts, and creates a much smoother user experience for learners.

    These updates are part of our mission to make Labiteers the most training-friendly cloud lab platform for educators, training providers, and bootcamps. If you’re running IT training labs and want a simpler way to manage student environments, we’d love to talk.
