Added support for Windows 11!

Published on Mar 18, 2026

We are excited to announce that we have added support for Windows 11!

To usitilise Windows 11 VMs in your labs, follow the instructions below:

  1. In your Microsoft Azure account, navigate to your Azure subscription, Settings, Usage + quotas.

  2. Increase the limit of your region’s quota.

Done!

Once your quota request has been approved, you can go back to Labiteers and spin up Windows 11 VMs.
